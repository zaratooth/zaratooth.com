Another End

about the project:

Another End is a procedurally-generated story-driven exploration game for the web browser built with optional controls for the Leap Motion and Oculus Rift VR.

But what does that mean? Every time the game is loaded the world gets pieced together, it isn't a premade world.

But wait the what about the story? The story is also procedurally generated. Us story writers have pre-written small events and tasks that the player interacts with and based on the player's actions the story changes.

It was a year long project with four other team mates, 2 programmers and 2 other design/asset/story creators.
